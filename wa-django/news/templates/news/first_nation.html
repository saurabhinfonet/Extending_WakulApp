The AIATSIS map of Aboriginal Australia

The Aboriginal Language Map attempts to represent all of the language or tribal or nation groups of Indigenous people of Australia. This map is based on language data gathered by Aboriginal Studies Press, AIATSIS and Auslig/Sinclair, Knight, Merz, (1996).
